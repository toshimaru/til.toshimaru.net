## [file fixture - RSpec Rails - Relish](https://relishapp.com/rspec/rspec-rails/v/5-0/docs/file-fixture)

```rb
require "rails_helper"

    expect(file_fixture("sample.txt").read).to eq("Hello")
  end
end
```
